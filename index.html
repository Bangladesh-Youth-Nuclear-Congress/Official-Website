What We Do

Fronts of impact

From a national innovation competition to a global delegation, BYNC builds the youth pipeline for Bangladesh's nuclear era.

Innovation for Nuclear

I4N Bangladesh 2026 — the national qualifying round of IYNC's flagship competition. 1 August 2026, Russian House, Dhaka.

Global Representation

As IYNC's 49th member nation, BYNC takes Bangladesh's first delegation to IYNC 2026 in Avignon, France.

Industry & Institutions

Bridging students with BAEC, NPCBL at Rooppur, Rosatom, Russian House and the worldwide nuclear network.

Knowledge Transfer

Campus sessions, international keynotes and workshops connecting youth to senior nuclear experts.
