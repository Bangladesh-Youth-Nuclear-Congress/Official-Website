A lot can happen in under a year

From a founding idea to a seat in the global nuclear network — here's the story so far, in the order it happened.

June 2026
Milestone

I4N Bangladesh 2026 is locked in

Over 500 students engaged and submissions rolling in across all three segments. Event day is confirmed: 8 August 2026 at Russian House, Dhaka.

January 2026
Keynote

Two more international keynotes for Bangladeshi students

Dr. Dinara Ermakova (IYNC Innovation Chair) and George Caliment (I4N Europe Lead, Assystem) addressed Bangladeshi students directly — on innovation and what it takes on the global stage.

December 2025
Programme

“Road to I4N” launches across campuses

Campus sessions kicked off at Dhaka University and MIST. BYNC was listed on IYNC's global Young Generation Network map, with a keynote from Alice Cunha da Silva (IYNC Vice President, Westinghouse).

August 2025
Membership

Bangladesh joins IYNC as the 49th member nation

The MOU with IYNC made it official — Bangladesh entered the global young generation nuclear network, and BYNC was featured on IYNC's official platform.
